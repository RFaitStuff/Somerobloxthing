# Story

## Core Premise

Humanity expanded while destroying itself. Five kingdoms ruled Earth, and their wars pushed civilization toward collapse. A hidden scientific order, the Star Eyes, believed machines would never be enough for survival in space, so they created The Pure: a dangerous adaptation serum and device.

Baldrin Vas survived open space after receiving The Pure. This event became known as First Breath. Baldrin was later found by Baya, an Atherin from the Asteri homeworld Atlas. Together, the Star Eyes and Asteri built warp gates across the solar system.

Earth fell into deeper conflict. The Star Eyes took control and became the Star Eyed. Humanity moved to the moon under a hidden dome colony called Moonveil. The Pure created the Adapted. The Asteri refined it into The Purest, creating unstable, powerful Altered.

The Kharons and other enemy origins reached the solar system. The Eclipsion destroyed Atlas. A young Atherin named Paie escaped with The Purest and eventually became Poise. Later, the Ryato found and destroyed Moonveil. Poise vanished with The Purest.

Years later, Poise returned to the ruined moon. She placed The Purest into a protected pond beneath a broken part of Moonveil. The dead glowed. Poise became still. The first Stained rose from the water.

The player is one of the Stained.

## Opening Flow

1. The player awakens as a Stained in the ruins of Moonveil on the moon.
2. They reach a temporary survivor base beneath a remaining protected dome segment.
3. They restore basic systems, recover a Vestige, and activate a damaged Moonveil warp gate.
4. The gate can only reach Earth at first.
5. On Earth, the player recovers salvage and Asteri gate components.
6. The player returns to Moonveil and uses the recovered parts to complete the Mars/Atlas gate link.
7. Completing the gate reveals the Atlas Shard pocket route.
8. The Atlas Shard becomes the central hub.
9. The player explores the Atlas Shard and meets the navigator, ending the prologue/tutorial.
10. Mars becomes the next major destination through the restored warp-gate network.

## Atlas Shard

The Atlas Shard is a surviving fragment of the Asteri homeworld, Atlas.

When The Eclipsion destroyed Atlas, one section of the planet containing an Asteri sanctuary and part of its warp-gate network was torn away instead of completely destroyed. Hidden inside a warped pocket of space that acts as camouflage unless approached closely, it remained undiscovered for years.

After the fall of Atlas and later Moonveil, scattered survivors, including Atherins, Star Eyed, humans, Adapted, and Altered, found the fragment and slowly rebuilt it into a hidden refuge. Using salvaged Asteri technology, they restored its systems and concealed it from enemy origins.

Today, the Atlas Shard serves as the last major safe haven in the solar system and acts as the central hub for survivors, traders, researchers, and Stained preparing to reclaim the worlds beyond.

## Moonveil Starter Structure

- Before the Atlas Shard is unlocked, Moonveil only exposes the temporary base/tutorial route.
- Extra Moon nodes exist in data but are hidden from the mission board until the hub is unlocked.
- After the player reaches the Atlas Shard, Moonveil opens back up as a returnable destination with Broken Dome patrols, crater salvage, and Ryato outpost missions.

## Important Terms

- `Atlas`: the Asteri homeworld, destroyed by The Eclipsion.
- `Atlas Shard`: a surviving fragment of Atlas containing an Asteri sanctuary and part of the warp-gate network.
- `Moonveil`: the ruined hidden moon colony where the Stained awaken.
- `Star Eyed`: the human successor order that rose from the Star Eyes.
- `Asteri`: the alien origin from Atlas.
- `Atherins`: near-human people of the Asteri.
- `Pure`: the original human adaptation serum/device.
- `Purest`: the unstable Asteri-improved version of The Pure.
- `Adapted`: humans changed by The Pure.
- `Altered`: survivors changed by The Purest.
- `Stained`: beings born from Poise, The Purest, and the dead of Moonveil.
- `Vestiges`: fragments of fallen Altered that Stained can use to gain powers.
- `Kharons`: an enemy origin connected to the destruction of Atlas.
- `Ryato`: an enemy origin that attacked Moonveil.
- `Eclipsion`: the massive weapon that destroyed Atlas.

## Gameplay Translation

- Moonveil is the tutorial/survival opening location.
- Earth is the first open patrol planet.
- Atlas Shard is the hub unlocked after restoring the wider warp-gate network.
- Mars is the first post-hub expansion planet.
- Quests describe story chains.
- Missions describe individual node activities.
- Story missions can run special variants of normal nodes.
